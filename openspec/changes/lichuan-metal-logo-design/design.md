## Design Direction Options

- Direction A: Modern monogram LM inside a steel shield. Clean lines, gradient steel blue palette. Suitable for header and favicon in compact sizes.
- Direction B: Iconic gear/hammer motif combined with a strong logotype. Conveys metal industry and robustness; adaptable to dark/light themes.
- Direction C: Pure typographic lockup with bold sans serif logotype. Emphasizes brand name and legibility across all scales.

### Brand System Notes
- Primary color palette: Steel Blue gradient (start: #2B5A83, end: #1E3A54); neutral grays for backgrounds.
- Typography options (headings/body): Option 1 - Headings: Montserrat, Body: Roboto; Option 2 - Headings: Poppins, Body: Lora. Both with safe fallbacks.
- Logo usage: Provide full-color, monochrome, and inverted variants; ensure clear space and minimum sizes for header, favicon, and social.

### Deliverables in this design phase
- 2-3 logo concept mockups (SVG) with export sets for header + favicon usage
- Typography and color tokens document
- Usage guideline for layouts, header integration, and iconography
