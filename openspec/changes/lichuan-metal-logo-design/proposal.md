Proposal: LiChuan Metal Logo Design

What and Why
- Objective: Design a new LiChuan Metal logo and establish a consistent branding mark to be used on the website header, favicon, social channels, and print collateral.
- Why now: A unified logo enables stronger brand recognition, improves visual hierarchy on the homepage, and supports scalable usage across devices and platforms.
- Scope: Create 2-3 logo directions (iconography + logotype or logomark-focused options), establish color system, and define usage guidelines for primary, secondary, and favicon applications.

Expected Outcomes
- 2-3 viable logo concepts with clear rationales.
- A lightweight color palette (primary, secondary, neutral) and font recommendations for headings/body.
- A concise usage guide indicating clear space, minimum sizes, and favicon handling.

Assumptions and Constraints
- Must work in both light and dark themes and be legible at small sizes (favicon, mobile header).
- Logo should reflect metal industry aesthetics (strength, durability) while aligning with LiChuan brand tone.
- Deliverables will include SVG vectors and raster previews (PNG/SVG) suitable for web and print.

Delivery Plan
- Create 2-3 concept directions in proposal review form.
- Shortlist by design review and proceed to design.md (how) with selected direction(s) in design strategy.
- Capture decisions and rationale to support rapid iteration.

Notes
- This document is self-contained and does not include internal constraints beyond branding goals.
