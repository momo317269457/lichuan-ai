# Hand-off Guide: LiChuan Metal Logo Design

- Purpose: Provide developers and designers with a clear handoff for logo usage, color tokens, and typography.
- Deliverables: logo assets (SVG/PNG), color-tokens.json, branding.css variables, usage examples.
- Setup: Import color tokens in the app's theme, deploy the header logo component, ensure favicon and social previews work with new assets.
- Accessibility: ensure logo remains legible in header across themes and sizes; provide alternative text for logo element.
- Review: Confirm with design lead before final deployment.
