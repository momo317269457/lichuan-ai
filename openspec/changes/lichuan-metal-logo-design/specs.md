## Logo System Specifications (Draft)

- Logo variants:
  - Full color
  - Monochrome (black on white) and reverse (white on dark)
  - Favicon version (simplified mark for 16x16/32x32)

- Clear space: minimum horizontal/vertical breathing room equals 1x logo width on all sides
- Minimum size: full color logo usable down to 24px height; favicon scales to 16px height
- Color tokens: primary steel blue gradient, secondary neutral grays, white for contrast
- Typography tokens (placeholder until official assets):
  - Headings: Montserrat (or Poppins) with bold weight
  - Body: Roboto (or Lora) for body readability
- Accessibility: ensure contrast ratio meets WCAG AA at all sizes
- Deliverables: logo.svg, logo.png, logo-favicon.ico, color-tokens.json, typography-tokens.json

- Review checkpoints: alignment with design.md concepts; cross-check with proposals
