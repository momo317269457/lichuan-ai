## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Dark industrial theme
The system SHALL use a dark industrial color scheme with metallic accents for the UI.

#### Scenario: Dark background applied
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** background uses dark gray tones (#0a0a0a to #1a1a1a)

#### Scenario: Metallic text colors
- **WHEN** text is displayed
- **THEN** headings use silver/light gray (#e5e5e5), body uses medium gray (#a3a3a3)

#### Scenario: Blue accent color
- **WHEN** interactive elements or highlights are displayed
- **THEN** accent color is blue (#3b82f6)

### Requirement: Modern card design with glass effect
The system SHALL use glassmorphism effect on cards with subtle borders.

#### Scenario: Card rendering
- **WHEN** cards are displayed
- **THEN** they have dark semi-transparent background, subtle border, and blur effect

#### Scenario: Card hover effect
- **WHEN** user hovers over a card
- **THEN** card lifts with shadow and border glow effect

### Requirement: Navigation and footer redesign
The system SHALL have a modern dark-themed navigation and footer.

#### Scenario: Navigation renders
- **WHEN** the header is displayed
- **THEN** it has dark background, sticky positioning, and proper spacing

#### Scenario: Footer renders
- **WHEN** the footer is displayed
- **THEN** it has dark background with proper text styling
